Faker - The legend of League of Legend

Lee Sang-hyeok, popularly known by his in-game name Faker, is a professional League of Legends player from South Korea. Widely regarded as the greatest esports player of all time, Faker's career is marked by numerous achievements and unforgettable moments that have solidified his legendary status in the world of competitive gaming.
